Digital Makers is one of the 7 work streams of LocalGov Digital and is a network for designers and developers in local government. The aim of the work stream is to encourage innovation and collaboration on digital between councils to create better user experiences and higher quality services.

Start and join in with discussions on Twitter, in the Google+ Community and on the Knowledge Hub.
